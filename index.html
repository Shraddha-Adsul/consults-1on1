Why This Exists

Over the past year, members of The Longevity Circle have consistently asked us for one thing: the ability to speak with a trusted doctor, one-on-one, outside the constraints of the traditional healthcare system.

We get it. A 15-minute appointment rarely leaves room for the questions that actually matter. Why is this happening? What can I do long-term? Is there a better path?

This offering is our answer. We built it to give you what the standard system doesn't: time, depth, and a physician who's genuinely listening.

How It Works

Getting started is simple. In just three steps, you'll be connected with a physician who's ready to give you the time and attention your health deserves.

1

Find your provider

Browse our curated list of providers organized by health concern. Every physician in our network was hand-picked for their clinical expertise and their commitment to thoughtful, patient-centered care.

2

Book a session

Select a consultation length that fits your needs — 15, 30, or 60 minutes — and pick a time that works for your schedule. Booking is quick and straightforward.

3

Have the conversation

Join your session with your questions ready. Our providers are here to listen carefully, offer clear guidance, and help you leave with a real understanding of your next steps.
