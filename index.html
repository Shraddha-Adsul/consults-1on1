Why This Exists

Over the past year, members of The Longevity Circle have consistently asked us for one thing: the ability to speak with a trusted doctor, one-on-one, outside the constraints of the traditional healthcare system.

We get it. A 15-minute appointment rarely leaves room for the questions that actually matter. Why is this happening? What can I do long-term? Is there a better path?

This offering is our answer. We built it to give you what the standard system doesn't: time, depth, and a physician who's genuinely listening.

How It Works

Getting started is simple. In just three steps, you'll be connected with a physician who's ready to give you the time and attention your health deserves.

1

Find your provider

Browse our curated list of providers organized by health concern. Every physician was hand-picked for their clinical expertise and commitment to thoughtful, patient-centered care.

2

Express interest

Select a provider. Expressing interest is quick and straightforward. Once you send us your details, we'll reach out with a payment link and coordinate available times to book your consultation.

3

Have the conversation

Once payment is confirmed, we'll book your session. Join the call with your questions ready — our providers are here to listen carefully, offer clear guidance, and help you leave with a real understanding of your next steps.

How We Chose Our Providers

We didn't build a directory. We built a shortlist.

Every physician in this network was selected because their philosophy of care aligns with ours — they believe health is an ongoing practice, approach medicine with curiosity, take prevention seriously, and make time to actually listen. These are doctors we'd recommend to our own families. Many of us already do.
